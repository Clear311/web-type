Inspired by the grandma feeding memes on the internet, “Stomach” is a variable typeface that highlights grandma's love for us. Each letter is designed to look like part of our stomach/intestines. As the times of visiting our grandma increase (which is the axis), the letters get blown up in both scale and width, symbolizing our stomach gets more and more bloated after our grandma feeds us. It took me 2 weeks to design the typeface so there are still many details to improve. To request a trial version of the typecace or to give suggestions, please email cshen03@risd.edu.
